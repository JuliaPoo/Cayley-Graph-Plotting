What are Cayley Graphs?

They're a visual for how elements in a group are related. Groups are elementary objects with loads of symmetry seen everywhere in Mathematics.

I'm currently working on a full blog post exploring these visuals. These visuals are usually not very useful and it's usually easier to work on groups abstractly, but I decided to make this little project to satisfy my curiosity.

I'll update this page once that's done!

What Groups are supported?

All groups of order 2 to 500 except:

  1. Most groups of order 128, 192, 288, 320, 432, 448 and 480.
  2. All groups of order 256 and 384.

There's just too many of them.
